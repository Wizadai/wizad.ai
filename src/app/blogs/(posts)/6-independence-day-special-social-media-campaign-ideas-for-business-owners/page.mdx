**Do you want to post creative and engaging content to promote your business this Independence Day; but don’t know what exactly you should post? Read along for some content ideas to market your product/service to your customers on Instagram, Facebook, Whatsapp, etc!**

Celebrated individually and commercially, Independence Day allows business owners to increase their sales through effective marketing. If you post customer-specific content for this upcoming Independence Day, you can also grow your audience just like your competitors! **Here, are some ideas and examples to boost your social media presence during special days like Independence Day:**

### **6. Independence Day Special Quiz**

Conduct quizzes on your Instagram story related to Independence Day. A fun quizzing series not only engages your audience in a fun time but also provides them with interesting information.

Suppose you’re a company having a market in Canada, you would want to celebrate Canada Day on July 1st. You can create special quizzes with questions like:

- Where in Canada is the locus of celebrations of Canada Day?
- Aside from English & French, what is the most spoken language in Canada?

### **5. Special Day Give Away posters**

Hold special events like lucky draws and giveaways on the occasion of Independence Day to attract more viewers to your page. Suppose you’re the owner of an electronics retail shop, you can give away an accessory under certain terms and conditions.

Let’s assume that you run a cosmetic store in Australia where you would want to commemorate its Foundation Day on 26th January. You can create a poster/reel which says that the giveaway winner will be chosen from ‘the people who follow your page and tag their friends in the comments’. By doing this, your account will reach many people who might be interested in buying your products.

### **4. Information Post regarding Independence Day**

Create posters that contain interesting information valuable to your customers, with regards to Independence Day. It should be catchy and easily hooking so that your audience lingers longer on your page.

Let’s say that you own a film school in the United States of America; you’d definitely want to celebrate the 4th of July. You can make information posters about the history and facts about America’s Independence and thereby promote your school/university’s Instagram (or any other social media).

### **3. Vibrant and thematic pictures of your product**

Upload photographs of your products in a way that aligns with the occasion, i.e., Independence Day, as well as your brand identity. The pictures should be of high quality and should communicate with your audience effectively.

Eg: If you’re a restaurant operating in the United Arab Emirates, then you might know how important National Day (2nd December) is for businesses. Upload appetizing pictures of your food items, that relate to the national occasion.

Let’s say that you’re a company where the majority of your product consumption comes from India. You can utilise the Indian Independence Day to increase your sales through promotion posters.
